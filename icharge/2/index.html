Why Areca Products?

Areca products are a new and blooming concept in today’s epoch. Being 100% biodegradable and eco-friendly, the areca products are slowly starting to replace paper and plastic wares. Made out of fallen sheath of betel-nut trees, the products are inherently designed for serving foods at various get-togethers and ceremonies especially in green weddings, birthday parties, temples, food corners, coffee houses and now the products are conceptualized to be used in corporate meetings as well. Replacing your tableware with areca products adds life to the earth and rejuvenates the environment.

About Bangalore Exports.

Far-famed as a reputed entity in the domain, Bangalore exports are highly acclaimed for manufacturing, and exporting the best quality range of disposable areca leaf plates. The plates are made available in various sizes and shapes like round, oval, heart, and rectangle leaving the customers with a variety of options to choose from. We also customize the products according to the customer’s requirement. We involve their ideas and designing concepts, to offer the right product pulled out of their imagination.

Serving as a perfect natural alternative for plastic and paper products, Areca leaf plates can prove to be a small initiative towards creating a pure and Eco-Friendly world. Driven towards creating a greener and safer world for the forthcoming generations, Bangalore exports are ardently engaged in offering affordable range of areca plates, crockeries, and cutleries with an excellent finish, and world class quality.
